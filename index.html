CurvedMe

emphasizing proportions while appreciating diversity

The Story

Near the end of 2019, we were approached by the owner of curved me with the desire to redesign his store.

After an in-depth discussion, we pinpointed a brand identity centered around relatedness, friendliness, and a general caring and welcoming persona, with a touch of alluring.

The challenge was emphasizing the benefits of the product while maintaining a positive body message in appreciating diversity.

Strategy

Based on the initial brand identity outlined we went for a pale and warm color palette with bendy shapes on most of the elements, and interactivity with the help of animated gifs and charts.

We adopted a story based flow through the landing-page with an understanding tone regarding the diversity of shapes, the story of the owner and the benefits of the products

Outcome

We tested our implementation of the initial concept with the help of a focus group. The website was characterized as welcoming, friendly, and inviting.

After the release of the website the owner saw a great increase of 50% in the conversion rate, which even exceeded our initial target of 20-30%, beeing a great success.
